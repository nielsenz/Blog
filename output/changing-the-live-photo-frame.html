Changing the Live Photo Frame

Posted on Sat 02 December 2017 in Tech Tips

One of the most requested features from Apple’s Live Photo is the ability to change what frame is displayed in the photo. Until I read this post from the Sweet Setup, I did not realize that it was possible to accomplish this.

All one has to do is to go into Photos, hit edit, and then by scrolling through the frames, a box that says “Make Key Photo” will be displayed. Super easy and useful, what a tip to dramatically increase the utility of Live Photos.
