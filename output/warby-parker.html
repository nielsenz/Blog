Warby Parker

Posted on Tue 07 November 2017 in Analysis

The Verge reports that Warby Parker is using Apple’s ARKit to help them size glasses on customers. The process of buying glasses at Warby Parker can be a painful back and forth process and this could help customers more easily visualize how the glasses will look on their faces. In July I discussed how Apple’s recently released ARKit could benefit apps on the iOS platform and Warby Parker looks like one of the first companies to put AR into production.
